National Pokédex number 0094: Gengar

Gengar is one of my favorite pokemon! He is a ghost/poison type from generation one which takes place in the Kanto region. He is often called the scariest pokemon of generation 1 due to his Pokédex entries

  • Under a full moon, this Pokémon likes to mimic the shadows of people and laugh at their fright

  • A Gengar is close by if you feel a sudden chill. It may be trying to lay a curse on you.

  • Appears to attack people who get lost in the mountains. Said to be the culprit behind shadows that laugh in the moonlight.

  • To steal the life of its target, it slips into the prey's shadow and silently waits for an opportunity.
