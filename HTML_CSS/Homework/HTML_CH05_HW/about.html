Should you feel yourself attacked by a sudden chill, it is evidence of an approaching Gengar. There is no escaping it. Give up.

Despite being one of the scariest people due to it's creepy Pokedex entries, Gengar is a fan favorite pokemon. They are available in every generation of pokemon! According to an entry from pokemon moon all Gengars were once humans. So it's not surpising they are in every region. The reason it takes lives is becasuse it wants a travel companion.

Gengar name is inspired from the word doppleganger. Which sometimes is interpreted as a spirit double. He is called the shadow pokemon and can go inside people's shadows and pretend to be them.

Gengar is the final evolution of a three level line starting with Gastly. Which evoles into Haunter who of course turns into Gengar. Gengar also has two special modes being a mega evolution and Gigantamax forms. These forms also solidfy Gengar as a a scary pokemon since in his Gigantamax form according to the Pokedex he becomes a gateway to the Afterlife.
